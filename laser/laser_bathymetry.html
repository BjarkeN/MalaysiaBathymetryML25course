Correct for refraction¶

We need to adjust the bathymetry for refraction. A simple correction for refraction is given as Z’ = Z+0.25416*D, where Z’ is the corrected elevation, Z is the uncorrected elevation, and D is the depth

In [105]:
# Correct for refraction
bath = depth - 0.25416*depth
plt.scatter(data[mask,0], depth, 0.01)
plt.scatter(data[mask,0], bath, 0.01)

# Compute the running median to determine the bathymetry
median_width = 200
running_median = []
for i in range(lat[mask].size):
    running_median.append(np.median(bath[i:i+median_width]))
